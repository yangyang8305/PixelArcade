About PixelArcade

PixelArcade is a free online arcade. Every game runs directly in your browser — no downloads, no accounts, no install. Just click and play.


Our Games

All games on PixelArcade are lightweight HTML5 games built and hosted by us. They work on desktop and mobile, load in seconds, and save your best scores locally in your browser.


How We Keep It Free

PixelArcade is supported by advertising. Ads keep the lights on so the games stay free for everyone.
